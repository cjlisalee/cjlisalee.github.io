Chi-Jung Lee | 李其蓉

I am a research assistant from Interactive Graphics Laboratory at National Taiwan University, supervised by Bing-Yu Chen. I am lucky to have the opportunities to work with Rong-Hao Liang at TU Eindhoven.I received my M.S. in Computer Science and Information Engineering from National Taiwan University and B.S. in Computer Science and Management and Technology from National Tsing Hua University.

My research interests focus on technical HCI, currently working on sensing and interactive systems to enable natural interaction and reduce the real and virtual world gap.
